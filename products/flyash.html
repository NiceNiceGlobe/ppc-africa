Classified & Unclassified Options

PPC offers both Classified (Class S) and Unclassified (Class N) Fly Ash products, providing engineers and builders flexibility in achieving high-performance, sustainable concrete and cementitious mixes.

Unclassified Fly Ash (Class N)

A granular product derived directly from the combustion process. It serves as a partial cement replacement, offering a slower hydration rate for improved workability and durability.

  • Alumino-silicates: SiO₂, Al₂O₃, CaO, Fe₂O₃
  • Ideal for structural and mass concrete
  • Reduces heat of hydration

Classified Fly Ash (Class S)

A finer ash selectively processed for superior particle size uniformity. Provides faster reactivity and contributes to early strength gain in cementitious systems.

  • High reactivity for accelerated performance
  • Enhanced long-term strength
  • Reduced permeability for durable concrete

Benefits

  • Cement extension to reduce clinker factor
  • Improves strength in bricks, blocks, and concrete
  • Enhances soil stabilization and road construction
  • Controls pH in agricultural applications
  • Suitable for mining rehabilitation
